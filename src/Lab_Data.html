Laboratory Data

This is the Laboratory Testing Data page. Jackson has no small amount of testing data, so I took the liberty of splitting it up into more digestable chunks based on usage, area, and/or testing. Please feel free to choose what you'd like to see.

Liquid Wastes

  • Dust Suppression Data Mud Dam testing data compared against license limits for Dust Suppression.
  • Drill Mud Composite Data (PFAS) Composite Drill Mud samples tested for PFAS contamination.
  • Stormwater Catch Dams Data Stormwater testing against Dust Suppression limits for Dams 6 & 7.
  • Landfill Stormwater Catchment Data Testing of landfill stormwater discharge for physical properties.
  • Effluent and Irrigation Data Testing of effluent (treated and incoming) against irrigation criteria.
  • Environmental Monitoring Data Environmental monitoring data for local surface water.
  • In-Situ Monitoring Data Surface water monitoring for fresh water sources and site water alike.
  • Oily Water Separator Data Testing data from liquid samples collected after processing through the Oily Water Separator (CURRENTLY UNAVAILABLE).
  • Bio-Remediation Rows Data Testing of bio-remediation material (CURRENTLY UNAVAILABLE).

Solid Wastes

  • Contaminated Soils Data Contaminated soils data, containing incoming, outgoing and interval testing data (CURRENTLY UNAVAILABLE).

Currently, these are incomplete, but more progress is being made daily, so in time you will have all the data you want!
